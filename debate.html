Security, privacy, and metadata at scale

The recurrent concert visits, the "Popstar Health Clinic -- Wig Department" visit... your senses are on high alert. You run a quick search, tracing patterns and dates until the truth snaps into focus. Riley Cyrus thought she could outsmart everyone by reinventing herself as Lannah Montanah—a secret agent and pop star undercover as, well, another secret agent and pop star! You definitely didn’t see this genius move coming!

Determined to uncover the truth, you decide to confront Riley in person. Disguised behind your trusty “newspaper-with-eyeholes,” you snag VIP tickets to Lannah Montana’s next concert and sneak into the backstage area.

When Lannah Montana finally walks by, shining under the glow of stage lights, you drop the disguise and step forward to confront her.

The following activity has no right or wrong answer. It's just an exercise for you to explore and challenge your views on the relationship between privacy and security.
