THE LEAK

You are newly assigned to the Bureau of Signals Intelligence. A data leak has triggered an emergency investigation. Your task is to analyze clues and metadata to identify who exposed classified operations.

Metadata is data about data: who created something, when, where, with which device, and how it moved across systems. Even when content is encrypted or hidden, metadata can still reveal patterns of behavior.

Mission format: interactive investigation files, network analysis, geolocation inference, and a final ethical confrontation.

Learning Goals

  • Build timelines from timestamps and technical traces.
  • Understand social graph analysis from call/email records.
  • Infer private routines from location points.
  • Debate surveillance ethics and accountability.
