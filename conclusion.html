Conclusion

Metadata is not harmless. It can expose identities, routines, affiliations, and vulnerabilities even when content remains hidden.

  • Document metadata can reveal origin stories and manipulation timelines.
  • Communication logs expose power structures and intermediaries.
  • Location traces reconstruct private life with alarming precision.
