CASE FILE 01 · DOCUMENT TRACE

Briefing

The story begins on a Monday morning.

You, Agent S. Carpenter, are called into the glass-walled office of Director Fenty. She slides a sealed note across the table:

TOP SECRET: INTERNAL LEAK DETECTED.

Classified files similar to the Snowden revelations have appeared on a whistleblowing site.

Your mission: identify the source.

You access an internal drive containing the leaked files.

They are logs, communication summaries, and reports. Nothing appears particularly incriminating, and you can’t trace a suspect based on the content of the documents alone.

So, you decide to look a the documents' metadata.

Mission

You direct your attention towards three files that seem to have a relation to the sandboxed workstation that the documents were leaked from: Virtual Station 04.

Process logs below show that each of the four cryptography‑lab users (Riley Cyrus, Belly Eilish, Lara Larsson and Whitney Dallas) ran sessions on that sandbox around the same window, and the timeline shows overlapping edit events from the same subnet.

  3. Select the primary suspect whose metadata best matches the leak sequence.
